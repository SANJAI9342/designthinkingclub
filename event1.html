Empathy Mapping


Define your target user group.Create a large, visual grid with four quadrants, representing “Says,” “Thinks,” “Does,” and “Feels.”Gather any available data on your target users and discuss insights with your team.Fill in each quadrant with observations and data that correspond to what the user says, thinks, does, and feels.Analyze the completed empathy map to uncover patterns or gaps in understanding to drive further research and ideation.


How to do it:


• Define your target user group.
• Create a large, visual grid with four quadrants, representing “Says,” “Thinks,” “Does,” and “Feels.”
• Gather any available data on your target users and discuss insights with your team.
• Fill in each quadrant with observations and data that correspond to what the user says, thinks, does, and feels.
• Analyze the completed empathy map to uncover patterns or gaps in understanding to drive further research and ideation.
